Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on the package selected. Basic setups usually take 24-48 hours, while Advanced and Full Setups can take 3-5 days.

Can you redesign my server?

Yes! I can take your existing server and completely transform it with professional layouts, roles, and systems without losing your current members.

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely. Your satisfaction is my priority. Each package includes a certain number of revisions to ensure everything is perfect.

Can you build any server type?

Yes, I have experience building servers for gaming, NFT/Crypto, educational purposes, businesses, and private social communities.

Do I need to invite you?

Yes, you will need to provide me with Administrative permissions or invite me to the server to perform the setup. I follow strict security protocols.

How do I get started?

Simply click the "Order Now" button, choose your package, and contact me via Discord or the contact form to discuss your requirements.
