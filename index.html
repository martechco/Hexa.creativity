Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on the package and complexity. Typically, Basic setups take 24-48 hours, while Full Setups can take 3-5 days to ensure every detail is perfect.

Can you redesign my server?

Absolutely! I specialize in taking existing servers and reorganizing them for better user experience, security, and professional appearance.

Can I request revisions?

Yes, all packages include revisions. I want to make sure you are 100% satisfied with the final result.

Can you build any server type?

Yes, I have experience with gaming communities, NFT/Crypto projects, business workspaces, study groups, and creator fan bases.

Do I need to invite you?

Yes, I will need 'Administrator' permissions temporarily to set up roles, channels, and bots. You can remove these permissions once the project is delivered.

How do I get started?

Simply click any "Order Now" button or join our Discord community to discuss your project directly with me.
